Core Research

Single Beam Gradient Force Optical Trap

Supervisor: Dr. V. R. Dantham, IIT Patna

Duration: Jan 2025 – Ongoing

Built a custom microscope to observe glass microspheres of size around ~12.2 μm and I am developing a 30 W CO₂ laser trap for nanoparticles using photonic nanojets.

Beaconless ATP

Supervisor: Dr. Pranav Pandey, Head- OCSD-QUTG/HSTA, Space Application Centre, ISRO

Duration: May 2025 – Ongoing

Developing a beaconless Acquisition, Tracking, and Pointing (ATP) system for optical satellite communication under ISRO guidance. The project focuses on probabilistic modeling, spiral scanning optimization, and system simulations to enable compact, power-efficient, and scalable optical links. Currently working on performance evaluation and system-level design for small satellite missions.
